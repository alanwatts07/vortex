# FINDING US

## OUR NORTH STAR

Human beings were never meant to walk past one another.

Finding Us is not designed to capture attention. It is designed to give
it back.

In a world competing for our time, our focus, and our attention, we are
choosing something different.

-   Less screen. More presence.
-   Less scrolling. More noticing.
-   Less performance. More humanity.

Turn your light on.

Look up.

Be present.

The technology creates the possibility.

The rest is yours.

## PRODUCT PHILOSOPHY

Finding Us exists to create a quiet invitation to look up.

There are no profiles. No followers. No swiping. No matching. No endless
messaging.

Just presence.

Turn your light on when you feel open to possibility. Notice who else is
present. Look up. Allow the moment to unfold naturally.

The app ends where human connection begins.

## THE LANGUAGE OF FINDING US

-   Light On = I am open.
-   Look Up = I am present.
-   Eyes Meet = I see you.
-   Smile = Connection is welcome.
-   Walk Forward = We begin.

No mutual eye contact simply means this moment wasn't meant for you.
Continue moving through the world with kindness.

## THE FINDING US TEST

Before implementing any feature ask:

> Does this encourage people to spend more time looking at their phones
> or more time looking at one another?

If it encourages users to remain inside the application, it is probably
the wrong decision.

If it encourages users to become more present with one another, it is
probably the right decision.

Finding Us is not designed to capture attention. It is designed to give
it back.
